COMING THIS SPRING

Credit for Life

Eight local banks who have long supported
financial literacy in Massachusetts schools
have formed a partnership to bring a
popular event virtually to students this
spring.

The Credit for Life Fair, a half day event
where high school students assume the
roles of 25-year-old adults and spend their
‘paychecks’ on everything they will need
to live, has been a popular event in many
Massachusetts public schools for more than
a decade.

We will be launching Spring 2021. Come back
soon!
